THE singer-songwriter and producer Tinashe made her debut with her album Aquarius in 2014.

In 2023, Tinashe's fanbase expanded after she joined the cast of the reality series Stars on Mars.

Who is Tinashe?

Tinashe Jorgensen Kachingwe goes solely by her first name, Tinashe.

She was born on February 6, 1993, in Lexington, Kentucky before moving to Los Angeles to pursue a career in entertainment.

The hit singer has a large following on social media with nearly 4million followers on Instagram alone.

In August 2021 she released her sixth studio album titled 333, after the angel number.

Prior to 333, she released the album Songs For You in 2019 which featured other artists such as the rapper 6LACK, Ms. Banks, MAKJ, and G-Eazy.

The 29-year-old artist revealed on her Instagram on March 22 that she will perform on The Kelly Clarkson Show on March 23, 2022.

In May 2023, Fox announced the cast of the new reality television series Stars on Mars.

Tinashe is on the show alongside other celebrities, including Adam Rippon, Lance Armstrong and more.

Who has Tinashe collaborated with?

Having been in the music industry for several years, Tinashe has worked with some of the biggest names in the industry.

A few of the famous artists she has worked with include Chance The Rapper, Offset, Ty Dolla $ign, French Montana, and Future.

She also performed at Lollapalooza in Chicago in July 2022.

What are some of Tinashe's top songs?

Tinashe has had much success with her music career.

She independently released her album 333 in 2021 after previously releasing her album Joyride under RCA Records, a division of Sony Music Entertainment.

She has landed on the Billboard 200 list several times with her album Aquarius spending three weeks on the chart.

Her top song on Apple Music is titled 2 On featuring the rapper Schoolboy Q.

Apple Music called her a "vibe setter" on her bio on the music streaming site.
