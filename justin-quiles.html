How did Justin start his Professional Career?

Quiles began his music career as a songwriter, writing songs for other singers before venturing out as a performer on his own. With his debut single “Orgullo” in 2012, he rose to prominence in Puerto Rico, earning him a record deal with Universal Music Latino.
