Early Life and Bio

Emmanuelle Chriqui, whose real name is Emmanuelle Sophie Anne Chriqui, was born on 10 December 1975 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada, as a daughter of father, Albert Chriqui, and mother, Liliane Chriqui. She has one older brother and one older sister as siblings whose names were Serge and Laurence respectively.

Movies and TV shows

Emmanuelle Chriqui started her career doing commercials for McDonald’s. She was also seen appearing as Jude Deshnell on a Canadian television series “Forever Knight”.
