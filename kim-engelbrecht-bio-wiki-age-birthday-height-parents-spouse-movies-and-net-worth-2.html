Kim Engelbrecht Biography

Kim Engelbrecht is a renowned South African actress famously known for her roles as Lolly de Klerk in the soap opera Isidingo, Sgt. Noma Banks on the Syfy TV show, Dominion, and Marlize DeVoe on The CW show, The Flash. Kim also presented a local youth TV show called Take5 in the ’90s.

Much of Kim’s work is localized in Johannesburg. She had a starring character in the acclaimed South African film Bunny Chow, directed by John Barker. She got her first big break at the age of 12 when she landed the lead character of Sara in an Italian movie, Sarahsara, in which she played the character of a 12-year-old girl of Sudanese origin who swims from the Isle of Capri to Napoli in Italy.
