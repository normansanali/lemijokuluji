Career

After working as a dancer and choreographer in several stage shows and schools, she got the opportunity to work as an assistant choreographer for the movie Chiranjeevi. Her notable works include the famous song ‘Dillaka Dillaka’ from the movie Racha in 2012, and in 2014, she received the chance to choreograph the songs of the Telugu movie Shankar Dada MBBS. After that, she was designated as an independent and senior choreographer of the Telugu film industry. She has choreographed the songs of more than 350 movies till 2015. She narrated her notable works with the prominent movies of the Telugu film industry in a conversation with a media house. She stated,

First was, Jyothi Lakshmi directed by Puri Jagannadh and then a song each in Rogue and Loafer and completed 30 songs in these two years. The Sardar Gabbar Singh project was a memorable feat for Anitha; getting an appointment with Pawan Kalyan is not easy — big stars have a comfort level with certain choreographers and seldom meet assistants.”

In 2017, Anee Master, along with her partner Priyamani, participated in the television reality show Dhee Jodi. Later, she received the opportunity to work on music albums. She also judged various dance reality shows including Dhee Jodi and Lil Kidz on Indian national television channels.
