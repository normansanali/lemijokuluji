Who Is Noah Flynn Aka Jacob Elordi?

Noah Flynn, real name Jacob Elordi, age 22, is an Australian actor known for his acting credits like the 2018 Netflix original movie The Kissing Booth, The Mortuary Collection, Swinging Safari, and HBO's Euphoria. 

Born in Brisbane, Australia, in 1997, Jacob Elordi celebrates his birthday on 26 June. He grew up with his two sisters, namely, Jalynn and Isabella, in his family and attended St. Joseph's Nudgee College in Brisbane.

On his physical attributes, Jacob has a decent height of 6 feet and 3 inches tall and a weight of 80 kg.
