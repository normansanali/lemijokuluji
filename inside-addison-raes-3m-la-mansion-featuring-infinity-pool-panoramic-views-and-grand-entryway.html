Inside Addison Raes $3M LA mansion featuring infinity pool, panoramic views and grand entryway

 Posted on September 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  553 words  |  Larita Shotwell

ADDISON Rae may have a budding career, though she’s already living large in her $3 million Los Angeles mansion.

The 20-year-old’s gorgeous home includes an infinity pool, panoramic views and a grand entryway.

The TikTok star’s home features six bedrooms, 7.5 baths and spans 6,399 square feet across 0.58 acres of land.

Every time Addison arrives home, she walks through the stunning set of double front doors.

According to Realtor.com, the LA home features a number of windows to let in natural light, while the TikTok star’s home also boasts a gourmet kitchen with top of the line appliances.

Meanwhile, the kitchen opens out into the large family room with fireplace.

The living rooms and large dining room are placed at the center of the home, while the spacious master suite includes a fireplace and balcony with amazing views of the large backyard and surrounding areas.

The home has a clean and welcoming feeling, which is created by the light open space and white furniture that invites guests to get comfortable upon entering.

The mater bathroom includes a spa tub, a double shower and a huge walk in closet.

Additionally, each bedroom has an en-suite bathroom so Addison’s guests will never have to worry about sharing restrooms.

The outside of the home is just as impressive as the inside, as Addison can spend time on the immaculately landscaped property with a huge backyard that offers plenty of room for laying out in the sun.

Addison and her guests likely spend a lot of time in the backyard, which includes a large pool and impressive views.

The 20-year-old rose to fame for going viral on TikTok, while she soon make her acting debut in the Netflix film He’s All That.

The movie- which is a remake of the 1999 film She’s All That- will debut on the streamer in August.

Outside of her career, Addison has made headline for her close friendship with Kourtney Kardashian, 42.
