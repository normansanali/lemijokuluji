Despite the fact that she left The Real Housewives of Atlanta before season 5, Kim Zolciak is still making headlines. Although her secretive relationship with "Big Poppa" was her most dramatic storyline on the Bravo reality series, her marriage to Kroy Biermann is what gets people talking these days. But while the couple once appeared to be totally in love, they have been in the process of getting divorced since spring 2023.

The couple has been dealing with terrible money issues for years now and Andy Cohen even said Zolciak spent too much money. The story of the couple's divorce keeps getting stranger. For example, in the summer of 2023, there was a false rumor that Zolciak kidnapped her and Biermann's son. Then Zolciak postedan Instagram photo implying that Biermann had died, which was perplexing.

In this article, we'll look at Kim Zolciak's Instagram post and her divorce from Kroy Biermann which doesn't seem like it will end anytime soon. We'll also take a look at why they chose to get divorced.

Inside Kim Zolciak And Kroy Biermann's Lengthy Divorce And Her Fake Death Post

While news broke about Kim Zolciak and Kroy Biermann's divorce in May 2023, Zolciak and Biermann are still married at the time of this writing, and their divorce is taking a long time. Zolciak's social media post that suggested that Biermann died was shocking, upsetting, and strange.

The Daily Mail reported that Zolciak shared a black and white photo of Biermann with "R.I.P." and a broken heart emoji on her Instagram account in April 2024. She also wrote "link in bio" and shared a giveaway that would allow her Instagram followers to get Chanel products or cash.

Since Biermann is still alive, this was an alarming and disturbing photo to see. It's hard to understand why someone would post that their husband and father of their children had died, especially since their older kids could see the post and get upset.
