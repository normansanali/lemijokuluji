Who is Nikki Glaser

Nicole Rene Glaser, professionally known as Nikki Glaser is an American comedian, radio personality, and television host who is currently the face of Welcome Home Nikki Glaser. 

Nikki Glaser: Net Worth

The TV star has an estimated net worth of $4 million. 

Nikki Glaser: Personal life

The TV star was born on June 1, 1984, in Cincinnati Ohio in the United States of America to Julie E Burke and Edward J. Glaser. The 38-year-old has one sibling called Lauren. She graduated from Kirkwood High School before furthering her college studies at the University of Colorado Boulder. She later transferred to the University of Kansas to complete her degree in English Literature. Nikki started her career as an entertainer, precisely a stand-up comedian in 2002 when she was 18 years. She subsequently became a host in the television series Nikki and Sara Live in 2013 and featured in the 2022 reality series Welcome Home Nikki Glaser. 

Nikki Glaser: Career

She started as a stand-up comedian in college. She proceeded to perform on The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, Conan, and the reality series Last Comic Standing. 

She was into podcasting as well. She hosted a podcast from 2011 to 2014 called You Had To Be There with Sara Schaefer. She went into a radio and TV career as well in 2013. She hosted the tv series Nikki and Sara Live in 2013. That same year, she appeared in the TV series Those Who Can’t. She has appeared in several other comedy series. 

Nikki Glaser: Awards & Achievements

In 2014, the comedian won the American Comedy Award for Best Club Comic. In 2015, she won the Breakout Artist of the Year Award at the Just for Laughs Festival and has won several awards as well. She won the 2017 Comedy Central Stand-up Showdown. She’s a multiple times nominee for the Primetime Emmy Awards. 

Who is Nikki Glaser’s Best Friend?

Nikki’s best friend from childhood is Kerstin Robertson. The two have a close bond. 

What is Nikki Glaser Known For?

Nikki Glaser, podcast host, stand-up comedian, and actress is popular for appearing on the reality series, Not Safe With Nikki Glaser and Welcome Home Nikki Glaser. 

Who is Nikki Glaser’s father?

Edward J. Glaser is the father of the American comedian. 

Does Nikki Glaser have kids?

No. The American star, Nikki does not have any kids. She is however dating the co-creator of Not Safe With Nikki Glaser, Chris Convy.
