How old is Joan Collins’ husband?

Percy Gibson was born in 1965 in Peru to his parents, Bridget Monaghan and Jorge Gibson Parra. As of 2023, he is 58 years old. Joan Collins is about 31 years older than her husband. Talking about their age gap, that actress revealed – “The fact that I was older didn’t matter to us,” she says. “It was giddy, it was fantastic and Percy and I fell madly in love, I’ve finally found my true soulmate. Per the Sun, Gibson hails from Peru and became a naturalized US citizen. Collins has shared that her husband is half-Peruvian and half-Scottish. In a 2018 writeup for the Daily Mail, Joan Collins talked about her husband, saying- “I’m not a Stepford wife and he’s an alpha-male Peruvian-Scot, so sometimes we clash but it doesn’t last long and we are quick to say sorry.“

Percy Gibson is Collins’ fifth husband

Before Gibson, Collins has been married four times. Her first marriage was to Northern Irish actor Maxwell Reed. In 1963, she married actor and singer-songwriter Anthony Newley, Collins wed her third husband, American businessman Ron Kass in 1972.

After Collins’ marriage to Kass ended in divorce in 1983, she married former singer Peter Holm, before she meet Gibson. Gibson on the other hand has also been married and divorced before he meet Collins. He was previously married to Cynthia Bauer from 1989 to 2001.

The actress recalled how she meet her current husband Percy Gibson in her 2018 essay for the Daily Mail. She wrote-“When Percy and I met 18 years ago, I was in a long-term relationship that was dragging along,” she wrote. “I had declared that I never wanted to marry again, and I kept to that promise until I met Percy … We hit it off immediately. He was funny, engaging, good-looking, and after the show I would socialize with him, my costar George Hamilton and my daughter Katy.“

After the play’s two-month tour, Percy Gibson and Joan Collins met up back in N.Y.C. and saw the musical Kiss Me, Kate. The following year, Gibson offered to help Collins type her fourth novel, and the two saw the chemistry and soon began their romance, she revealed to the Daily Mail in 2013.

The two love birds tied the knot in a beautiful ceremony at Claridge’s Hotel in London on Feb. 17, 2002, in the presence of their friends, families, and loved ones. During the wedding ceremony, the bride ditched the traditional wide dress and opted for a lilac silk-crepe gown by Dynasty designer Nolan Miller.

Talking about why they don’t have any children together and also talking more about their age gap, the actress revealed- “I was in my 60s and he was in his 30s, but the age difference never posed a problem,” Collins told The Guardian in 2013. “We talked it through and he didn’t want children. He adores my children and grandchildren. We’ve been married for 11 years, but when I look at him across a room, my heart still skips.“
