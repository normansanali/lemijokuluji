Rose State College Shooting: Who is RJ Long?

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  258 words  |  Martina Birk

One person was killed and a suspect was arrested after a shooting Monday at a community college just outside Oklahoma City, officials said.

Rose State College, which is about 8 miles east of downtown Oklahoma City, issued its first active shooter warning at 12:36 p.m. CT.

Midwest City Assistant Fire Chief Anthony Lopez said one person was dead in the shooting.

Students first reported gunfire from the Humanities Building a little after 12:15 p.m., a school spokesperson said.
