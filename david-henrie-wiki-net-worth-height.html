David Henrie is a well-known American performer, director, writer for television, and producer. Henrie became well-known for playing Justin Russo in Wizards of Waverly Place and Luke, the prospective son of Ted Mosby, in How I Met Your Mother. He has also starred in several other movies, such as Little Boy and Wait Until Mickey, which have increased his fame.

Where was David Henrie born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

On July 11, 1989, David Henrie was born in Mission Viejo, California, in the United States. His mother, Linda Henrie, and his father, James Wilson Henrie, are the parents of David Henrie. His father is a director, and his mother oversees talent.

Lorenzo James Henrie, a younger brother who is also an actor, is his only relative. David Henrie is of mixed ancestry; on his father’s side, he has English, Welsh, German, and Swiss-German forebears, while on his mother’s side, he has Italian ancestors. He was brought up in Phoenix, Arizona, and was a devout Catholic. He’s made it clear that he’s a Believer and always will be.

Is David Henrie Single? Relationship

David Henrie is a happily married man who identifies as straight. The Wizards of Waverly Place co-stars were reunited at Henrie’s nuptials to Maria Cahill on April 21, 2017, marking their long-awaited reconciliation.

James Thomas Augustin Emanuel, their son, and Pia Philomena Francesca, their daughter, are the couple’s two offspring. Henrie and his wife made the news of their third child’s impending arrival in December 2021.

How tall is David Henrie? Weight, Hair Color

As of 2022, David Henrie, who was born on July 11, 1989, will be 33 years old. He stands 1.8 meters tall and weighs 77 kg.

How did David Henrie start his Professional Career?

When it comes to his work, David Henrie got his start at the age of 13 after snagging a regular part in the Fox comedy The Pitts. He played the part of Petey Pitt in this comedy. After that, he made appearances in movies like Monster Markers, Dead Hollywood Mothers Society, and Hallmark Movie.

The Mullets, Jack & Bobby, The D.A., House, NCIS, and Cold Case are just a few of the other shows he has featured on. He played Ted’s future son in How I Met Your Mother and Cory’s friend Larry in That’s So Raven, respectively, and had recurring parts in both shows.

When he was 18 years old, he portrayed Justin Russo in the Disney Channel Original Comedy Wizards of Waverly Place. His popularity increased considerably as a result of this show. Meet the Werewolves and Alex’s Logo, are two segments of this program that he also wrote.

Later, he acted in films like the Disney Channel Original Movie Dadnapped, Jonas, Easy to Assemble, The Secret World of Arrietty, Grown Ups 2, 1000 to 1: The Cory Weissman Story, Mind Games, and he also produced two short films called Catch and Boo! in 2014.

Later films he appeared in included Little Boy, Walt Before Mickey, Cardboard Boxer, Warrior Road, Paul Blart: Mall Cop 2, and Walt Before Mickey. He also produced the movie This Is the Year in 2018. He and Selena Gomez, his co-star on Wizards of Waverly Place, confirmed the release of a virtual movie titled This is the Year in the year 2020.
