Did boresome conquer your daily life during the quarantine session? Well, you don't have to worry. Even in the isolation period during the world pandemic Coronavirus, many artist have tried their best to pour the sparkles of entertainment to most of us.

And the recent announcement of country singer Garth Brooks about his upcoming tour added some flavor to it. The country singer announced that he would be performing at a drive-in theater on 27 June 2020, and that day is not far from now. The concert also has the best part to come in. It will air live at 300 drive-ins across the nation.

Garth appeared on 'Good Morning America' with his announcement and mentioned that his upcoming concert is the reason to get out of the home that you've been quarantining for some months. In the meantime, the social distancing guidelines will be followed at the concert. Garth said:

"We are excited because this is a reason to get out of the house, but at the same time you get to follow all the COVID-19 rules from every individual state and you get to have fun and stay within the guidelines of social distancing ... we're calling it 'social distancing partying."

Well, ready your seat belt and catch your snacks for Garth Brooks' performance on 27 June. Tickets will be available after 19 June 2020, and it will cost $100. The hosts will announce the Drive-ins concert sites on 15 June 2020.
