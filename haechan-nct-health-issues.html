Haechan (NCT) Health Update As The Band Suspends Promotion

 Posted on August 29, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  692 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Haechan’s health situation and the fact that he had been given a rest break were both explained in a statement issued by NCT’s management company, SM Entertainment, on January 6.

NCT Haechan Suspends Its Schedule Due To Heart Palpitations

Concern among fans has been raised by news that SM Entertainment has stated that NCT member Haechan will be suspended from performing due to health reasons. On the afternoon of January 6, SM Entertainment announced that NCT Haechan will temporarily halt the schedule so that he could take a break and receive medical attention.

The NCT won’t be able to work with Haechan on any activities the following week because of his absence. NCT’s hectic schedule during their current promotional period has caused concern among fans. The entire declaration is as follows:

“Hello.

The health of member Haechan is being reported, as is his absence from the schedule.

Haechan had been having heart palpitations and chest pain, so he went to the hospital with his boss for a checkup and obtained the proper care. Haechan will miss appointments in order to rest and recover because it is crucial for him to get enough sleep.

As a result, Haechan won’t be present for the next week’s schedules. We will announce when he will resume his activities at a later time. We apologise for worrying Haechan’s supporters and kindly ask for your understanding regarding his well-being.

We will put a lot of effort into helping Haechan heal and come back in good health.

I’m grateful.

Recently, Haechan began to feel symptoms of an erratic heartbeat and shortness of breath. His manager took him to the emergency hospital, where he had a consultation and medical examination.

He is so instructed to take some time off work and rest so that he can heal. Next week’s group meetings are scheduled, but Haechan in particular won’t be there because of his vacation.

NCT 127’s Latin American and US Tours Will Not Feature Haechan

On the same day that DREAM MAKER USA announced her absence, Haechan was also informed that he would not take the stage at the next Latin American and US tour legs of NCT 127. NCT DREAM recently received promotion from Haechan as a result of the group’s recent release of the new remake of Candy.

NCT 127’s repackaged album Ay-Yo, which will be released later this month, will be the group’s return. The NCT has encountered this situation before, with a member missing time due to illness. Mark Lee mentioned his tiredness during the group’s comeback, despite the fact that Jaehyun had already experienced the same issue.
