Artem Chigvintsev's Net Worth

Artem Chigvintsev has amassed the net worth of $6 million through his professional career of dancing. After moving to the United States in 2003, he started dancing with the champion ballroom dancer Gisele Peacock. 

He also auditioned for the first season of So You Think You Can Dance, where he got eliminated. In the second season of the show, he made his return as a choreographer. Similarly, he appeared in the dancing reality show Dancing with the Stars. 

In 2010, he competed on Strictly Come Dancing Season 8, where he partnered with actress Kara Tointon and won the show. Similarly, in the ninth, tenth, and eleventh season of Strictly Come Dancing, he was paired with Holly Valance, Fern Britton, and Natalie Gumede.

More About Artem Chigvintsev

Artem Chigvintsev, whose full name is Artem Vladimirovich Chigvintsev, was born in Izhevsk, Russia, on 12 June 1982. He moved to Germany at the age of 15 to pursue dance training. He later moved to Italy, England, and finally the United States before he paired up with his ex-girlfriend, Gisele Peacock.

Talking about his body measurements, he has a chest size of 45 inches, arms of 16 inches, and waist of 33 inches. Similarly, Chigvintsev measures at the height of 5 feet and 10 inches tall with a weight of 80kg.
