Biography:

Megan Boone was born on April 29, 1983 in Petoskey, Michigan, U.S. She attended Florida State University’s School of Theatre where she completed her BFA in Acting in 2005.

She also studied with Jane Alexander at the Asolo Repertory Theatre, and workshopped with playwright Mark Medoff. In 2001, she made her screen debut with the short film Elijah.

She has since appeared in other films such as My Bloody Valentine 3D, Sex and the City 2, Step Up Revolution and About Cherry. As of 2013, she plays Elizabeth Keen on the TV series The Blacklist.
