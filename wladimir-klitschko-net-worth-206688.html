Wladimir Klitschko, born on March 25, 1976 in Semey, Kazakhstan, is a former heavyweight champion who won a gold medal at the 1996 Olympics in the super heavyweight class. He became a dominant force in the heavyweight division in 2005, winning the WBA, IBF, WBO, IBO & The Ring Heavyweight Championships. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest heavyweight boxers of all time.
