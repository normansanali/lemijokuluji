Oprah Wears This Comfy, On-Sale Loungewear So Much, She Owns 12 Sets

 Posted on July 5, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  458 words  |  Martina Birk

Oprah is an expert on many things, but if I had to choose just one thing, I’d say she’s truly a pro at loungewear. Without a shadow of a doubt, Queen O knows what sets a good lounge set apart from a great one, so whenever she recommends anything that’s “cuddly,” you know it lands on my wishlist — especially while it's on Cyber Monday sale. 

To no one’s surprise, Oprah’s Favorite Things List for 2023 was chock-full of winners. There was beauty! There was home! And, of course, there was loungewear! While some might have stopped mid-scroll when they came across cuddly set #1 (aka, the Spanx AirEssentials two-piece that made a return this year), I stopped when I spotted this Stripe and Stare sweatshirt and sweatpants combo that looks so comfortable and so chic — chicfortable? I’d never heard about the brand, but obviously, if it caught Oprah’s attention, it was worthy of mine, but the biggest selling point? The fact that she owns 12 (!!!) sets. 

“I have three of these sets in green, three in pink, three in white. And now I’ve added three more, in almost purple — lavender!” told Oprah Daily. Now, if someone owns two of the same piece, you know it’s good. But if someone owns 12 of the same thing? It must be out-of-this-world amazing, and to say I was extremely intrigued would be an understatement. I need to know what these are all about!

Comfort aside, this Oprah-favorite — and soon to be your favorite — also seems to be highly versatile, as in, there are endless occasions to which you can wear it, per Oprah. “I wear them as pajamas. I work out in them. I wear them all the time!” Whether you’re sleeping or breaking a sweat, this Stripe and Stare combo will support you along the way, and honestly, when you’re shopping for loungewear, versatility is a key component that sets one, well, set apart from the other. 

The loungewear is made from a cloud-like, tencel fabric that’s comfier than cotton and more sustainable (a win-win!). It's lightweight yet buttery soft, and that’s exactly why it’s an ideal ‘fit for so many occasions. It’s currently available in two colors, a soft purple and a classic black, and in sizes small to 4X. 

The cherry on top? It’s on Cyber Monday sale! The lounge set normally goes for $160, but right now, it can be yours for $128 — which means each piece costs $64 a pop. I’m adding to cart as I type because if Oprah has 12, I must have at least one, right? Right.
