John Deere 3020 is a row crop tractor that was built by John Deere as part of its New Generation series from 1964-1972.

The John Deere 3020 row crop tractor is nearly equal to the John Deere 3010 model that it replaced. It has a fuel capacity of 29 gallons and a wheelbase of 90 inches.

It was offered three different engine options, including a 4.4L 4-cyl diesel, a 3.7L 4-cyl gasoline engine, and a 3.7L 4-cyl LP gas engine, all produced by John Deere. The John Deere 3020 was rated at 71hp horsepower.

Transmission options included the standard Synchro Range transmission that gives eight forward speeds and two reverse speeds and an optional eight-speed powershift transmission system.

John Deere offered several different tread options on the John Deere 3020, including narrow front Row Crop with “Roll-O-Matic,” wide front Row Crop, standard, narrow front Row Crop, Row Crop Utility, and Orchard.
