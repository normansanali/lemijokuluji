Tim McGraw tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

 Posted on September 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  392 words  |  Martina Birk

TIM McGraw is kicking off his 2022 tour on April 29, and we've got all the info on tickets and locations.

The tour kicks off on April 29 in Rogers, Arkansas, and wraps up on June 4 in Mansfield, Massachusetts, before McGraw begins making appearances at several festivals.

Where is Tim McGraw performing?

The official McGraw Tour will be live in the following cities:

  • April 29: Rogers, Arkansas
  • April 30: Maryland Heights, Missouri
  • May 5: Jacksonville, Florida
  • May 6: Tampa, Florida
  • May 7: West Palm Beach, Florida
  • May 12: Charlotte, North Carolina
  • May 13: Alpharetta, Georgia
  • May 14: Orange Beach, Alabama
  • May 19: Cuyahoga Falls, Ohio
  • May 20: Camden, New Jersey
  • May 21: Raleigh, North Carolina
  • May 26: Syracuse, New York
  • May 27: Holmdel, New Jersey
  • May 28: Bristow, Virginia
  • June 2: Hartford, Connecticut
  • June 3: Wantagh, New York
  • June 4: Mansfield, Massachusetts

How do I get tickets to see Tim McGraw?

While it's worth checking against third-party apps and sellers for tickets to his concerts and passes to the music festivals where he'll be performing, we've found the most reliable source to be directly from Tim McGraw's website.

This will provide you with the links for the venues themselves.

Who else will be performing with Tim McGraw?

Russell Dickerson, Brandon Davis, and Alexandra Key will be opening for Tim McGraw's 2022 tour.

You can find information as it is released about who will be singing with him at the festivals on his website.
