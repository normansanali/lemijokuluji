Wiki/Biography

Floris Damen, popularly known as Fundy, is a Dutch YouTuber who was born on Wednesday, 10 October 1999 (age 21 years; as of 2020) in the Netherlands. Fundy started uploading videos on YouTube in 2019. Initially, he started posting short clips from Minecraft but soon, he began uploading full gameplay and going live on the channel to stream. He also started streaming on Twitch, an online video streaming platform.
