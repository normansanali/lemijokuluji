Olivia Cohen

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  460 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Olivia is a blogger, playwright, and actress. She has been performing since the age of twelve and has studied at the Lee Strasberg Institute in New York, Stagedoor Manor Theater Camp, UCLA, USC, Magie Haber Studio, Anthony Meindl Actor Workshop, and seven years of secret instruction with Tanya Berezin.

Olivia has a BFA in Screen & Television Writing from the University of Southern California School of Cinematic Arts and has written for over ten feature films, a couple pilots, and a novel. Her dramedy feature, This Was Fun, was named a Fellowship of Academy Nicholls semifinalist in 2018.
