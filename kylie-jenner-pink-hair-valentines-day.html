Kylie Jenner Wore Pink Hair for Valentine's Day 2016 with Tyga

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  169 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

As the 500th piece of evidence that Kylie Jenner loves playing around with beauty more than any of her sisters, we present her hair color choice for Valentine's Day: pink. For a date with Tyga that kicked off with a helicopter ride, the world (a.k.a. her Instagram followers) got a glimpse at a new, holiday-appropriate mane. The raven-black hair she's been showing off at Fashion Week was gone, replaced by warm pink waves that perfectly echo the color of her new lip kit shade. Like many of her hair switches in the past, this one isn't permanent but rather the handiwork of stylist (and wig master) Tokyo Stylez. He's an asset Kylie has utilized before, and she shares him, with Stylez helping Kim Kardashian West pull off the white-blond look she debuted at Kanye West's fashion show last week.

So, what's your verdict?
