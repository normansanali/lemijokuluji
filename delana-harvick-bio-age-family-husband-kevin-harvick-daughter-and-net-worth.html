DeLana Harvick Biography

DeLana Harvick is an American Businesswoman who was born and brought-up in Kernersville, North Carolina. She is widely known as the wife of Kevin Harvick, the NASCAR Cup Series driver. She is the Former Co-Owner and Manager of Kevin Harvick Incorporated, a racing team in NASCAR’s Nationwide and Camping World Truck Series.

The Kevin Harvick Incorporated (KHI) was later sold to Richard Childress, who renamed it to Richard Childress Racing. She has worked in public relations for driver Randy LaJoie and Jeff Gordon. In 2006, she was honored as one of the four “Sensational Women” by the Hershey Company for her support in breast cancer awareness.
