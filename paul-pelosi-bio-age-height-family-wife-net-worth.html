Biography About Paul Pelosi

Paul is an American businessman who was born in San Francisco. He is the youngest among his siblings. He went to school at St. Ignatius High School and completed at the Malvern Preparatory School in Pennsylvania. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Foreign Service at Georgetown University. He received his MBA from New York University’s Stern School of Business. Since 2009, he has presided over Georgetown’s Foreign Service Board.

Paul Pelosi Age

Paul Pelosi is 82 years old. He was born on April 15, 1940 in San Francisco, California, USA.

Paul Pelosi Career

Over the years, Pelosi has developed into a prosperous businessman. His entrepreneurial activities include owning and running Financial Leasing Services, Inc., a real estate and venture capital investment and consulting organization based in San Francisco.
He made an investment in the USFL’s Oakland Invaders, a professional American football team, but it disbanded in 1985.

In 2009, he spent $12 million to acquire the United Football League’s (UFL) “California Redwoods” team. The squad moved to Sacramento later and started playing in October 2009. Pelosi oversaw the Georgetown University Foreign Service Board in 2009 as its chair. He serves on numerous business and charitable boards.

He has significant holdings in equities of Walt Disney, Shutterfly, Apple, Facebook, and Comcast, among others. A real estate business in California called Russell Ranch LLC, where he is also believed to have invested, is said to have added $4 million to his net worth.
